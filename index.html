What is Freemasonry?

One of the oldest fraternal organizations in the world, uniting men of good character who share a belief in the fatherhood of God and the brotherhood of mankind.

Discover More Contact Us

Understanding Freemasonry

Freemasonry offers a system or blueprint for the good man to further improve his character and actions through its system of degrees, symbols, and fellowship opportunities.

🏛️

When did it start?

Modern Freemasonry was established in England in 1717, with roots tracing back to medieval stonemason guilds.

🌍

Worldwide Presence

6 million Freemasons worldwide, with 1.1 million in North America. Over 60,000 Masons in Ohio alone.

⚜️

Not a Religion

Freemasonry does not replace faith, family or occupation. It reminds every Mason of his duty to the Supreme Architect.

The Three Degrees

The journey through Freemasonry is marked by three ceremonial stages representing youth, manhood, and age.

1ST DEGREE
Entered Apprentice

The first step as a Freemason. A candidate's initial experience with the ceremonies of the Fraternity.

2ND DEGREE
Fellow Craft

Progress from apprentice to journeyman, learning more of the symbolism and philosophy.

3RD DEGREE
Master Mason

Full membership with all the rights and responsibilities of the Fraternity.
